Mutes a given user. You can also specify a time-limit, after which the user will be automatically unmuted by the bot.
**Syntax:** mute (user_mention) (optional: time) (optional: unit)
**Example Usage:** mute @Azrael#4441 30 m
**Valid Units:** s (seconds) and m (minutes)

If you're specifying a time and unit, make sure there's a space between time and unit (e.g. it's 30 m instead of 30m)
